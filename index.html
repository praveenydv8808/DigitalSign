DigitalSign

Welcome to the Digital Signature Tool

Instructions:

  • Click and drag on the canvas to draw your signature.
  • Use the color picker to select a color for your signature.
  • Use the font size selector to choose the size of your signature.
  • Click the "Eraser" button to activate the eraser. Click again to deactivate it.
  • Use the eraser to remove parts of your signature.
  • Click the "Clear" button to clear the entire canvas.
  • Click the "Download Signature" button to download your signature as a JPEG image.
